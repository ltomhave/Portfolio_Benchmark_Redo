Animal Help Now

UI|UX Designer and Project Manager

THE OVERVIEW:

Coordinate with nonprofit leaders and production team on creating and launching an IOS application for a new arm of the nonprofit. This new arm, Pet Help & Rescue, aims to provide users with a way to quickly save pets in case of a disaster by reaching out to a trusted contacts with the personal information to get to your animals.

TOOLS USED:
Illustrator Figma Github

PROJECT OVERVIEW

THE GOALS:
• To create a mobile application (IOS and Android) that aims to provide users with a way to quickly save pets in case of a disaster by reaching out to a trusted contacts with the personal information to get to your animals • To launch a new branch of Animal Help & Rescue
THE CHALLENGES:
• Coordinating with a team that though talented are primarily volunteers and work different hours
• Learning curve for design andd developers based on the IOS shift to Swift platform.
• Creating a great product on a limited budget
THE TEAM:
This is comprised of:
• Two UI|UX designers
• One Product manager
• 6 Developers
CURRENT STATE:
• Currently the application is prototyped and we are trouble shooting any code or ux challenges for the launch with engineers
• Prepping to beta test a MVP product
